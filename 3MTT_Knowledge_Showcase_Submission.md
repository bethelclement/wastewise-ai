**Project Name:** WasteWise AI  
**Focus Pillar:** Environment (with strong Digital Inclusion)  
**Live Demo:** [https://wastewise-ai.vercel.app](https://wastewise-ai.vercel.app)  
**GitHub Repo:** [https://github.com/bethelclement/wastewise-ai](https://github.com/bethelclement/wastewise-ai)

### 1. The Real-World Problem
Across high-density areas in Abuja, urban waste management is heavily reactive. Environmental agencies wait for residents to file slow complaints or wait for designated, inflexible pickup days. This creates temporal gaps where critical public health hazards (like illegal sludge dumping or overflowing organic decay) fester and cause illnesses like malaria or cholera.

### 2. The Users
1. **The Residents (Households):** Everyday citizens who need a rapid, inclusive, and localized digital flow for logging waste situations.
2. **The Agency (Dispatch Supervisors):** Municipal workers who need to know exactly which geographic zone is collapsing immediately so they can route trucks efficiently. 

### 3. What Was Built (The MVP)
WasteWise AI is a unified web application split into a **Civic Reporting Flow** and a **Smart Prioritization Dashboard**. Using responsive UI logic and serverless architecture, a resident can categorize a waste incident and an area urgency. The MVP immediately processes the incident via the dashboard, turning raw subjective inputs into mathematical risk matrices.

### 4. Impact (The Value Proposition)
By moving from reactive scheduling to predictive risk-routing, WasteWise AI allows contractors to save heavy operational fuel costs while protecting communities from severe public health hazards. High-risk zones are mitigated *before* overflow cascades into an emergency.

### 5. Sustainability & Scalability
The underlying architecture operates on modern, extremely lightweight React edge networks leveraging a scalable Prisma ORM database pipeline structure. It requires no physical sensors, IoT hardware, or massive field deployment costs. A local agency can adopt the dashboard overnight with zero capital hardware expenditure, relying purely on crowdsourced resident data.
